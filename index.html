From Dentist to AI-Enabled Program Leader

I didn't start out in tech. I'm a Doctor of Dental Surgery from India, and my journey into program management wasn't planned — it was a pivot driven by curiosity and a love for building scalable systems.

Over the past 11 years, I've led enterprise-scale technology programs across healthcare, retail, telecom, and cloud infrastructure. My dental background gave me something unexpected: deep listening skills, empathy for diverse stakeholders, and the ability to work under pressure while maintaining precision. I gained regulatory discipline (HIPAA compliance), attention to detail in complex environments, and the patience to explain technical concepts to non-technical audiences. These human-centered skills became a real competitive advantage.

Now I'm merging everything — my healthcare knowledge, 11 years of execution excellence, listening and empathy skills, and a newfound passion for AI and intelligent automation. I'm fascinated by how Gen AI can make program delivery more predictable, more efficient, and less about firefighting.

This portfolio shows how I'm evolving — taking what I've learned building impossible programs with people at the center, and exploring what's next with AI.
